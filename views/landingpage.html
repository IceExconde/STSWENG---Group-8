OUR FOUNDERs

On August 1, 2022, Padua Realty was founded by Reyma G. Padua, a Certified Housewife with three beautiful kids and who is also a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) by profession. Mrs Padua, understood the importance and need for every Filipinos to have a place to be called a home and this realization led her to aspire to contribute towards this basic need for shelter at a reasonable cost and terms.
